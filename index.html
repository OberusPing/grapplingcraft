The Grappling Craft

A digital garden for coaches who care about the craft of teaching grappling: ecological dynamics, constraints-led practice, skill acquisition, training design, and the lived texture of the room.

The aim is not to collect techniques. It is to collect better ways of seeing practice, shaping problems, and helping athletes become adaptable under pressure.
